Full Stack Developer
--------------------

Hello, I am Kaan Bolat, I graduated from Eastern Mediterranean University, Department of Computer Engineering. I completed the semesters by receiving the Certificate of Honor in 2019, 2020 and 2021. I had previously done a 3-month voluntary internship at ARKSOFT company located in Hacettepe University Teknokent. Apart from this, I worked on Microsoft Office programs for 2 months at the Fe-Ni mining company. I am interested in the game servers designed by Vice Gaming, the young entrepreneurs project we created together with our circle of friends. Later, I worked full time as a Computer Engineer for 6 months at a company called FocusGIS. I made improvements to the TAD and TED portals of the Ministry of Agriculture. I took part in the development of the application called KolayMAP. Finally, I started working at a company called Siren Bilişim. I continue to develop Eczacıbaşı's Evital, Securitas' Recruiement, Ministry of Health's Library Management, Contract Management, AkınLAB's portal, PeakSmart, and Cyprus Vaccine portal. I use Angular and .NET in all projects.

* 🌍  I'm based in Turkey
* ✉️  You can contact me at [okaanbolat@icloud.com](mailto:okaanbolat@icloud.com)
* 🚀  I'm currently working on [Siren Bilişim&Yazılım](https://www.sirenbilisim.com.tr)
* 🧠  I'm learning react, flutter, vueJS
* 🤝  I'm open to collaborating on any angular and .net projects
